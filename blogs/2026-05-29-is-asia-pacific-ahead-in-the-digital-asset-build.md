69% of APAC financial institutions are already live or running client-facing pilots for digital assets. APAC’s 2026 deployment numbers do not reflect a sudden acceleration. They reflect years of deliberate investment in capability and laying institutional groundwork, achieved through dedicated innovation labs, specialist hiring, regulatory sandboxes, and infrastructure experiments.
