Fireblocks has launched Flow, a new stablecoin acceptance infrastructure designed to help payment service providers and fintechs easily integrate cryptocurrency payment capabilities. The platform simplifies the previously complex process of accepting stablecoins by handling wallet connectivity, asset conversion, compliance verification, and automated reconciliation in one integrated solution.
